I'm an interdisciplinarian with a background on arts, creative technologies and critical thinking. I live and work in Berlin.

Currently I'm creating a videogame together with my friend pabloselin.

During 2004-2015 I created video and installation art with my friends as INCAS OF EMERGENCY.

I've done sound design and music for audiovisual projects, such as this, and this. You can find my music on Spotify, Soundcloud or Bandcamp.

I've been a teacher of technologies for the arts and design at UVM, UNIACC and ARCOS in Chile. There I was able to teach from creative coding with Processing to several tools of the Adobe Creative Suite. I also got to guide Visual Arts students in their Bachelor Thesis process.

I have been a designer at plinga, home24 and bookingkit.
